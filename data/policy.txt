# Acme Corp HR Policy Handbook

## 1. Remote Work Policy
Acme Corp supports flexible work arrangements. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days a week with approval from their direct supervisor.
To request remote work:
1. Discuss with your manager.
2. Submit the "Flexible Work Arrangement" form in the HR portal.
3. Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a secure workspace.

Full remote work is available for specific roles and requires Department Head approval.

## 2. Travel Reimbursement
Employees traveling for business are entitled to reimbursement for reasonable expenses.
- **Flights**: Economy class for domestic; Business class for international flights over 8 hours.
- **Meals**: Up to $75 per day.
- **Lodging**: Standard hotel rates (up to $200/night).
Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of travel via the Expense system.

## 3. Paid Time Off (PTO)
- **Vacation**: 15 days per year for first 3 years; 20 days thereafter.
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year.
- **Holidays**: Acme Corp observes all federal holidays.
Unused vacation days can roll over (max 5 days) to the next year.

## 4. IT Security
- All devices must be encrypted.
- Do not share passwords.
- Report phishing attempts to security@acmecorp.com immediately.
- VPN must be used when accessing company resources from public Wi-Fi.
